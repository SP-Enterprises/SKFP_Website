FIRE ALARM SYSTEMS

The supply and installation of a fire alarm system includes providing all necessary components such as smoke and heat detectors, alarm panels, manual call points, and notification devices. The system is designed to detect fire or smoke and trigger audible and visual alarms to alert occupants. Installation involves positioning detectors and alarms according to safety standards and the building layout.
